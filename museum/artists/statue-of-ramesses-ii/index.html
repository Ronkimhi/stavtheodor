Statue of Ramesses II

ancient Egyptian red granite statue found in 1882 which is presented nowadays in the Grand Egyptian Museum of al-Giza, Egypt, c. 1250 BCE

The Statue of Ramesses II is a colossal 3,200-year-old figure of Ramesses II, depicting him standing. It is 11 metres (36 ft) tall and made from red granite, weighing about 83 short tons.
